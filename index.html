what's new

  • The Korea-India Quiz was held in 2021.

    The students of class xii had their second session with the students at ulsan foreign language high school, korea. The korean students were at their school while their indian counterparts were at home due to the current lockdown. A captivating korero on "movies" produced in both nations, india and south korea, was held. Attendees from both the sides engaged in an enthralling discussion. Even the teachers ms. Meenu Kumar and mr. Joon Son joined the discussion and talked about formula of ‘masala movies’. The talks varied from some indian golden oldies to the sensational-oscar winning movie-slumdog millionaire to some korean blockbusters like parasite. Both the teams gathered a new perspective about films as an art form .The students also recommended movies to one another . Few ufl school students also gave brief presentations touching upon the two major movie genres : bollywood and k-pop. the session culminated with a small question and answer session.
